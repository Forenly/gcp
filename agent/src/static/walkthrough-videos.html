Why Trupeer, not a re-record

Re-recording with a mic means a clean take, a script, manual edits and zoom work. Trupeer does all of that in one pass — and hands you a written guide on top. It closes the exact gap (no narration) without the manual effort.

Definition of done

v2 is done when…

  • Narration on every step — no silent stretches
  • Script reviewed & correct (names, actions, intent)
  • Split into chapters that map to real steps
  • Smart zooms point at the action on each click
  • Both the video and written guide exported & shared
  • A teammate can follow it cold — no live narration from you
